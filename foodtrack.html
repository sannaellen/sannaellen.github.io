FOOD TRACK

Food Track wants to show the full journey of the product. With this application it becomes possible to see a visual overview of how the product has been transported, as well as get more information about both human and animal conditions at, for example, conditions in factories and farms. Food Track is an informative and learning application that aims to broaden knowledge about the food consumers choice to take more responsibility and to make a more active choice.

This project is a design process of a prototype to treat the topic of sustainability and the environment. The prototype has had a user-centered design process with multiple iterative moments as well as an underlying focus on critical design. The prototype has been named Food Track and is in the form of a mobile application developed from many brainstorming meetings, prototypes, user tests and re-designs.

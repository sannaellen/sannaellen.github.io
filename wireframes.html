WRIEFRAMES

 Is a visual guide that represents a basic framework of a website, an app or any other digital interface. Wireframes are created to try out and to find the best sollution for your interface. Wireframe usually lack any typographic style, color, or graphics, since the main focus lies in functionality, behavior, and priority of content.

Wireframes - ecommerce platform

B2B ecommerce

During my internship i had responibilities in doing wireframes for B2B ecommerce platforms. By poing this, and by presenting this to customers it was possible to easily start a discussion of what they want to keep and what they want to take away. 

Wireframes - Mobile site

Mobile site for B2B ecommerce

By also including a wireframe for the phone application you can plan for a responsive webpage. For examle in this case, the products were put into a list on the web page and on the mobile site it would be in a grid view. The list would maybe not be as good in the mobile site version, due to lack of space.

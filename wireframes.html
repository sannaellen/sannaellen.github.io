SOME OF THE WIREFRAMING TOOLS THAT I USE

ILLUSTRATOR

To develop a more detailed wireframe, a hi-fi wireframe, Illustrator can defInitely do the work! I also enjoy doing graphic designs in Illustrator and therefore, I feel comfortable working with wireframes in this program. 

BALSAMIQ

When wireframeing in Balsamiq you can really get the flow started and make easy but well thought out ideas for many different platforms. I like that you can work cooperatively in a document, that really makes it one of my favourite wireframing tools. 

OMNIGRAFFLE

I often use Omnigraffle as a tool to create content maps, screen flows, and wireframes. I enjoy the vector design and the drag and drop feature makes it a fast program to do wireframing in. 

AXURE

Axure has relevant built in icons, it is also a good program to make prototypes that can be user tested due to easy animations that show how different feautres will work and get the perspective of a working program.

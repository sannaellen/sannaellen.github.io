ECOWALLET

During my studies in advanced HCI I created a critical design project. I decided to do a lo-fi and hi-fi prototype of my concept, Ecowallet. By gathering knowledge about the target groups values and opinions through interviews, the critical design artifact Ecowallet was created.

THEMATIC ANALYSIS

A thematic analysis can be a way of analysing qualitative data. By connecting answers from the interview you could find out different themes that were found to be common with other users. In this case, the thematic analysis found the themes that were beneficial to understand the main points of what users feel when consuming and what could make them consume less.

INTERACTION DESIGN

An interaction designer understands, designs and analyzes the interaction between human and technology. It's important to have a good ability to see how people use their technology, both professionally and in everyday life. It is therefore primarily about creating products that users love to use.

I chose to study interaction design, because I´m interested in cognitive behavior and I want to develop solutions for the future. I have a passion for designing clean and functional user experiences and i love turning complex problems into beautiful and intuitive interface designs.

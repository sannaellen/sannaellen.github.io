Intelliplan - UX designer

Intelliplan is a business system for staffing companies with business areas within staffing, finance, recruitment and sales.

At Intelliplan I work with creating the best user experience for new and existing users by conducting user tests, surveys, creating and letting users click test prototypes and having an iterative design process. I have a close relationship with our users and develop the system together with them.

Design System

A Design System is like a lego block library of elements in a system. It´s a way of making reusable parts that can fit in to multiple places in your system. I´ve been a part of the process of creating these reusable components to speed up the design process but also the development.

MOBILE FIRST DESIGN

If you start by designing for the mobile end product design, under the restrictions that a phone has, you can naturally seize the key points of a product. When the platform is expanded to a tablet or PC, you are able to take advantages of the unique features of these advanced ends to strengthen the product step by step.

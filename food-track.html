A CRITICAL DESIGN PROTOTYPE

This project is a design process of a prototype within the topic of sustainability and the environment. The prototype has had a user-centered design process with multiple iterative moments as well as an underlying focus on critical design. The prototype has been named Food Track and is in the form of a mobile application developed from many brainstorming meetings, prototypes, user tests and re-designs.

SEE HOW YOUR FOOD HAS TRAVELLED

Food Track wants to show the full journey of the product. With this application it becomes possible to see a visual overview of how the product has been transported, as well as get more information about both human and animal conditions at, for example, conditions in factories and farms. Food Track is an informative and learning application that aims to broaden knowledge about the food consumers choice to take more responsibility and to make a more active choice.

PLANNING

During a planning session an overview of the process was made. This was later made in digital form so everyone in the design group could easily get hold of it. 

LO-FI PAPER PROTOTYPE

A lo-fi prototype out of paper was made to easily make design changes and try out different ideas. 

DESIGNING THE DIGITAL
HI-FI PROTOTYPE 

A digital hi-fi prototype was developed from many iterations in Illustrator and in Proto.io. Several of user tests were done during both the paper prototype, lo-fi digital prototype and on the digial hi-fi prototype to make the finished product easy to use and what the users expect from the product.
